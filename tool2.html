Alloy Payload Analyzer

Instructions

  1. Obtain the Alloy event payloads JSON (e.g., from sessionStorage or another source).
  2. Copy the JSON array of events.
  3. Paste it into the textarea below.
  4. Click "Parse Payloads" to analyze the events.
  5. Use the filter to search for specific fields or values.
  6. Click on "[Object]" links to expand/collapse nested details.
  7. Click "Download as JSON" to save the payloads.
