About Me

As a Martech Engineer at CVS Health, I specialize in building data-driven, privacy-compliant solutions that power enterprise-scale personalization. My expertise spans Adobe Experience Platform (AEP), Web SDK, and Customer Data Platforms (CDP), delivering:

  • Identity Resolution: Seamlessly unifying ECID and first-party IDs for cohesive customer journeys.
  • Edge Computing: Optimizing server-side tagging for performance and privacy.
  • Tag Management: Creating scalable architectures with Tealium iQ and Adobe Launch.
  • Privacy & Compliance: Ensuring GDPR and HIPAA-compliant data pipelines.
  • Experimentation: Driving A/B testing and analytics with real-time insights.

My solutions blend engineering precision with analytics expertise, delivering measurable impact through open-source-friendly architectures.
