About Me

I'm a Martech Engineer with expertise in crafting data-driven, privacy-compliant solutions for enterprise-scale personalization and analytics. At CVS Health, I lead initiatives in Adobe Experience Platform (AEP), Web SDK, and Customer Data Platforms (CDP), focusing on:

  • Identity Resolution: Unifying ECID and first-party IDs for seamless customer journeys.
  • Edge Computing: Leveraging server-side tagging for performance and privacy.
  • Tag Management: Building scalable solutions with Tealium iQ and Adobe Launch.
  • Privacy & Compliance: Ensuring GDPR/HIPAA-compliant data pipelines.
  • Experimentation: Powering A/B testing and analytics with real-time data.

My work bridges engineering and analytics, delivering measurable outcomes through robust, open-source-friendly architectures.
